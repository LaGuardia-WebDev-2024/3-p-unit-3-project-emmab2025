About Emma

Emma is 17 years old and is a versitile dancer. She currently attends LaGuardia highschool as a senior in the dance program, is a MOVE NYC Young Professional, and also attends Jump Dance studio in Rockaway Beach Queens. She has also been selected to perform in the LaGuardia Dance Ensemble at her school from 2023-2025. She has Peformed For Earl Mosleys diversity of dance at dancing beyond, Dance against cancer, and the Apollo Theater for the Manhattan Borough Arts Festival.
